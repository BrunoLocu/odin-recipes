Strawberry Rhubarb Crisp

This strawberry rhubarb crisp is perfect for those who wonder what to do with that big rhubarb plant in the garden. This recipe is your answer â€” it will have you coming back for more!

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • White sugar
  • Flour
  • Oats
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Step 1: Make the first layer with fruit, sugar, and flour. Place in the baking dish.
  2. Step 2: Make the topping, then sprinkle it over the fruit layer.
  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is crisp and lightly browned.
